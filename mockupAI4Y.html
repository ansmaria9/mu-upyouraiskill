Axioo AI Summit 2024

Axioo AI Summit 2024, held on January 21-22, 2025, is the culmination of the Intel Digital Readiness collaboration between Axioo Indonesia and Intel Digital Readiness. Serving as the grand finale of the Axioo AI Skill Contest, which took place from December 23, 2024, to January 4, 2025, the summit features finalist teams presenting and demonstrating their innovative AI projects to a panel of judges. This event offers teams the chance to showcase their solutions, explain their development process, and highlight the potential impact and implementation of their work. Supported by Axioo, Intel Digital Readiness, the summit not only celebrates the best AI solutions from students but also underscores the commitment of the education and industry sectors to developing digital talent in Indonesia. The summit emphasizes collaboration, aiming to foster the growth of Indonesia's AI and technology ecosystems.
